Bridging Design Intent & Cost Reality

Aether Rigden Consultants provides specialist façade cost consultancy and pre-construction support for main contractors, architects, and developers. We focus on the commercial and technical aspects of façade systems, helping project teams ensure façade designs are deliverable, commercially viable, and aligned with project budgets.

Our deep expertise across early design stages and tender development allows us to provide insight where cost certainty is most valuable.

Specialist Services

01

Façade Cost Consultancy

Commercial advice for fenestration systems, External Rainscreen systems and complex cladding architectures.

02

Pre-Construction Estimating

Feasibility budgets and early-stage cost planning to guide design evolution. Technical advice on what products and systems suit project design and purpose.

03

Tender Support

Subcontractor pricing reviews and detailed commercial comparisons. Assist with inclusions on early design stage to avoid scope gaps when forming budgets.

04

Recladding & Remediation

Specialist cost planning for façade replacement and safety works programmes.

05

Budget Cost Planning

Façade system benchmarking, value engineering, and detailed cost modelling.

06

Scope Quantity/Item Take-Offs

ARC delivers detailed façade measurement and item schedules to support accurate take-offs. By identifying elements often missed during early estimating, we provide clear quantities and a comprehensive façade component list.

07

ARC Façade Tender Proposal Pack

Includes: full take-off, item schedule, risk notes, value engineering opportunities.
