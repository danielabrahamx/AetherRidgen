Website Design Brief
Aether Rigden Consultants Ltd (ARC)
1. Company Overview
Aether Rigden Consultants (ARC) is a boutique façade cost consultancy providing specialist
pre construction and cost advisory services for external wall systems. The consultancy supports main
contractors, architects, developers, and project teams by providing early stage façade cost advice, tender
support, and procurement insight. ARC focuses on the commercial and technical aspects of façade
systems, helping project teams ensure façade designs are deliverable, commercially viable, and aligned
with project budgets. The brand should feel professional, specialist, and high end, similar to boutique
construction consultancies.
2. Website Objectives
• Present ARC as a specialist façade consultancy
• Appeal primarily to main contractors and architects
• Communicate technical expertise and commercial understanding
• Act as a credibility platform for future clients
• Provide a simple way for clients to get in contact
• Remain minimal and professional – ideally a one page or simple multi page layout
3. Target Audience
Primary: Main Contractors, Architects
Secondary: Developers, Project Managers, Design Teams
These users are professionals within the construction and development industry.
4. Website Tone & Style
• Professional
• Technical and knowledgeable
• Modern and minimal
• Architectural in feel
• Boutique consultancy positioning
• Avoid sales heavy language or anything resembling a subcontractor website
5. Proposed Website Structure
Hero Section
Headline example: Precision in Façade Cost Planning
Aether Rigden Consultants provides specialist façade cost consultancy and pre construction support for
main contractors, architects, and developers.
Call to action: Discuss Your Project / Contact ARC
About Section
Introduce ARC as a boutique consultancy specialising in façade systems and external wall construction.
Highlight experience in early design stages and tender development and explain how the consultancy
bridges the gap between architectural design intent and contractor cost reality.
Services Section
• Façade Cost Consultancy – cost advice for curtain walling, glazing systems, and cladding
• Pre Construction Estimating – feasibility budgets and early stage cost planning
• Tender Support – subcontractor pricing reviews and commercial comparisons
• Recladding & Remediation – cost planning for façade replacement and safety works
• Budget Cost Planning – façade system benchmarking and cost modelling
Why ARC Section
• Specialist façade expertise
• Strong understanding of façade supply chains and pricing structures
• Independent consultancy advice
• Focused on pre construction stages where cost certainty is most valuable
Clients Section
ARC works with main contractors, architects, developers, project managers, and design teams across
projects including commercial developments, residential towers, and façade remediation programmes.
Contact Section
Simple contact area including email address, contact form, and optional phone number. Primary call to
action: Discuss your project with Aether Rigden Consultants.
6. Brand Direction
Brand Positioning
ARC should be positioned as a specialist boutique consultancy rather than a contractor. The brand should
convey expertise, precision, and clarity in complex façade systems.
Suggested Colour Palette
Colour
Dark Charcoal / Graphite
White / Off White
Muted Steel Grey
Accent Bronze or Subtle Blue
Typography Direction
Purpose
Primary brand colour – professional and architectural
Clean backgrounds and readability
Secondary colour for UI elements
Optional highlight colour for premium feel
• Primary font: Modern sans serif (examples: Inter, Helvetica Neue, or similar)
• Large bold headings for architectural feel
• Clean readable body text
• Minimal use of decorative fonts
7. Image Direction
• High quality architectural photography
• Modern glass buildings and façade systems
• Close up façade details (glazing, cladding, structural elements)
• Abstract architectural textures
• Avoid generic construction site imagery where possible
8. Example Websites for Inspiration
• Wintech Engineering – façade engineering consultancy
• Eckersley O'Callaghan – structural and façade engineering consultancy
• Arup – engineering consultancy design style
• These examples demonstrate clean, technical consultancy branding.

Aether Rigden Consultants is a specialist façade cost consultancy helping project teams make better
